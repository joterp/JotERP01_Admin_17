Legend :-
BHC: Brake holding capacity (Tons) As per Manufacturer
BRL: Set Brake Rendering Load (Tons) On the Winches
MBL: Maximum Braking Load of Rope
Pd: Reference Pressure to Applied in the Hydraulic Jack to perform the Test
Pa: Actual Pressure on gauge(Kg/cm²)When Brake Slippage occurs
A: Cross sectional area of hydraulic piston(cm²)
L: Distance measured from the center of the winch drum to the center line of the Hydraulic Jack Piston (cm)
D: Diameter Mooring Drum (cm)
d: Diameter of the Rope (cm)
Procedure :-
1 - Fill Yellow Cells
2 - Start building the pressure progressively up to the design reference Pd
3 - Record Actual Pa at the Hydraulic Jack at the first sight of movement of the drum
4 - Mark the Brake Spindle of winch for rendering point with Cable Tie
